How it works

Three pillars. One movement.

01
🌿
Adopt a green space
Youth groups officially claim a neglected park. Name it, design it, program it. Pride + ownership = real care for the city.
02
🎯
Weekly green missions
Social challenges tied to real locations in Kaunas. Always group-based. Build sustainable habits through joy, not guilt.
03
🏘️
Neighbourhood councils
Youth-led groups with real decision-making power. Monthly meetups build lasting belonging across districts.
Green entrepreneurship

Don't just use the space.
Build something in it.

Green spaces become economic hubs. Youth stay because they have opportunity — not just parks to sit in.

☕
Pop-up cafes
Youth-run coffee stands in adopted green spaces. Low startup cost, high foot traffic from weekly missions and events.
🌿
Urban gardens and markets
Grow and sell local produce, herbs, flowers. Weekly green markets create recurring income and community ritual.
🎨
Workshops and classes
Outdoor yoga, art, cooking, repair cafes. Sell your skills in a unique open-air setting that draws a crowd.
🛠️
Space maintenance jobs
Paid roles to maintain, design and upgrade adopted spaces — funded by city partnerships and business revenue.
